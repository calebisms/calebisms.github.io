  • Do your services include fact checking or plagiarism checks?

    I don’t have the tools to do plagiarism checks. I might be able to help with fact checking depending on what field the work relates to. Just contact me to discuss!

  • Can you provide faster delivery?

    I can usually provide 24-hour delivery, especially for smaller projects for those only requiring proofreading. For smaller jobs, it’s US$10-20 or A$15-30 extra. Simply indicate that you need the service when you contact me and I'll confirm whether I can do it!

  • How do I pay?

    I’ll send an invoice when the work is completed, and you can pay by card.

  • Can you guarantee that your edits will bring me fame and fortune?

    Unfortunately, no.
