  • Do your services include fact checking or plagiarism checks?

    I don’t have the tools to do plagiarism checks. I might be able to help with fact checking depending on what field the work relates to. Just contact me to discuss!

  • How long does your service take?

    Three days is the standard delivery time on most small orders. Many larger orders (over about A$300 or US$200) take 1-2 weeks. Before starting on your work, I'll provide an estimated time frame and check that this will work for you.

  • Can you provide faster delivery?

    I can usually provide 24-hour delivery, especially for smaller projects for those only requiring proofreading. For smaller jobs, it’s US$10-20 or A$15-30 extra. Simply indicate that you need the service when you contact me and I'll confirm whether I can do it!

  • How do I pay?

    I’ll send an invoice when the work is completed, and you can pay by card.

  • Do you offer discounts for larger projects?

    I do! For projects of 10,000 words or over, I'm usually able to offer at least a 10% discount. Contact me for more info.

  • What time zone are you in?

    In September 2023, I'm Pacific Daylight Time. That's 7 hours ahead of Australian Eastern Standard Time, 9 hours ahead of Singapore time, and 8 hours behind the UK.

  • I'm not sure which service to choose, can you help?

    To explain our three pricing levels succinctly, a basic proofread is a check for typos and grammar mistakes. A standard edit will also make sure each line reads naturally, is consistent with other content, and doesn't repeat ideas. Finally, a detailed edit looks at the text as a whole, rewriting and rearranging as needed for a fully polished piece of writing. If you're still unsure, feel free to send me a sample of your work and I can show you what the different levels of editing would look like on it.

  • Can you guarantee that your edits will bring me fame and fortune?

    Unfortunately, no.
